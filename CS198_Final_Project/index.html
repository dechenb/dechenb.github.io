California Sea Lion

California Sea Lions are found from British Columbia to Mexico, favoring the California coast.

They belong to the order Pinnipedia, a clade consisting of seals, sea lions, fur seals and walruses.

As part of the Otariid Family, California Sea Lions are distingushuable from other Pinnipeds by their external ears, or pinnae, and their ability to use their front flippers to sit upright and “walk”.

They are social creatures and you will often find them sunbathing on haul-out sites or on land. Great spots to find California Sea Lions in the bay area are at Fisherman’s Wharf in SF and Monterey Bay.
